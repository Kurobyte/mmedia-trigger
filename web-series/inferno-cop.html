History

The story deals with those in this world who dwell in the dark, invisible to the naked eye. From time to time, these figures bare their fangs and attack people. Inferno Cop is a messenger of justice who came from the depths of hell to protect people from them.

Background Information

This was the first "long" project of the studio. It was made simultaneously when the studio was working on Little Witch Academia, which came to cinemas after the end of Inferno Cop.

Also they made a extra short and realy funny videos talking about the characters called Inferno Cop Fact File.
